The Lonely Little Pea

Illustration + Typography

Description

The Lonely Little Pea is a childrenâ€™s story book I had written when I was 11 years old for a computer class. The original was my first experience creating computer based illustration, and typographic layout. It was the first spark toward my future in graphic design.

Design Solution

I took the opportunity to utilize my tactile skills and create another marketable item: the plush toy. This lead to the design of a tag, which was sewn on as well.

The original book needed some simplification. White space was added for breathing room and the type was re-written and arranged for ease of readability. Illustrations were given more depth of context and storyboarded to reflect the new flow of the narrative. The use of bold coloured characters against a mild background allows the characters to pop, as they are the focus of the story.
