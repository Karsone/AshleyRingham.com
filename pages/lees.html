Lee's Donuts

Branding + Identity

Description

Lee’s Donuts is a donut shop located in Granville Island Marketplace, a popular attraction for locals and tourists alike. Lee’s is one of the oldest establishments still located there today, over 35 years, and they have gained quite a following of loyal customers. Lee’s still holds true to their values: being family run and their natural handmade, donut making process.

Design Problem

Lee’s has a great reputation for their product, however, they lack a strong brand identity. Their branding is dated and their web presence is virtually non-existent. Most of their publicity has come from publications, TV spots, word of mouth and user operated pages like yelp, which all have great ratings. They have a clear, strong following that needs to be communicated with in order to strengthen the brand.

Design Solution

Top priorities of the re-brand was to foster a strong connection with the segmented audience groups by implementing a web presence, as well as to create brand awareness and recognizability.The audience wants the branding to reflect the happiness of the product, therefore the lighthearted feel created with rounded, bold shapes and friendly lettering was created. The yellow-orange colour was chosen based on the famous hot honey dip donut.
