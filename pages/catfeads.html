Cat Cafe Ads

Illustration + Photography

Description

Catfe is a newly opening cat cafe in Vancouver. Cat cafes first became popular in Japan, now the trend is spreading across the world. Cat cafes operate as a regular cafe, but you can play with cats as well as enjoy typical coffee shop treats and beverages.

Design Problem

Catfe has not opened yet, and is having trouble finding a location, the aim of these ads is to entice and excite the audience’s curiosity and get involved in the opening of the cat cafe.

Design Solution

Catfe’s existing logo was used in the ads. Cats playing with and around a mug, demonstrate the feeling of being around cats while drinking coffee. The target audience knows how cats act and react to things, and this gives them an emotional connection with the advertisement. The cats are illustrations, cut out of paper and photographed because this makes them look animated, and gives them a facade of realism.
