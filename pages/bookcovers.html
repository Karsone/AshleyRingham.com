Children's Book Covers

Illustration + Typography

Description

The three stories all have a few things in common, they take place in different magical lands: Wonderland, Neverland and Oz, and at one point the characters float in spaciousness: Peter Pan flies, Dorothy is swept to Oz in a tornado, and of course Alice falls down the rabbit hole. This gives the stories a bond that makes it easy to create a set.

Design Solution

The character illustrations are modeled after simple paper dolls, and are set in white space seemingly falling or flying, depicting their individual parts of the story, while showing their connection as a set. Elements of the book float around the characters to hint at other aspects and props of the story. The letters in the title also float and fall to mimic the illustration. The covers simplicity will appeals not only to children, but to adults, as they may be the ones to buy the set.
