Bold Nail Polish

Branding + Packaging + Mobile UI/UX

Description

Bold Nail polish is a fictional nail polish brand that I created. I wanted to create a series of bold colourful packaging, along with patterns made from line icons. These icons are illustrated to reflect the names of the nail polish. The identity for Bold Nail polish is all about fun colours and bold statements.

Design Solution

The logo is kept simple with bold typefaces and thick lines, this is because the logo must hold up and be visible on the small packaging. The line icon patterns are a continuing element across all platforms, print and devices. The brand is playful but the logo stays black and white to show off the colours of the nail polish which change across branding elements. A mobile application lets you photograph an object and shows you a colour polish best suited to match. This allows the user to perfectly match different elements of their outfit to their mainicure.
