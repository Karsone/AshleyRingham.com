The Little Red Apple Tea Co.

Packaging + Typography

Description

Little Red Apple Tea Co. is a fictional company selling apple themed tea products. Since this is a fictional company, the identity needs to start from scratch. The branding and packaging needs to reflect the simple farm style of the company, while staying fun and charming.

Design Solution

The design uses kraft paper with different colour gingham patterns and dark wood for the crates, making the packaging rustic and homespun. A larger crate is used to house the teapots and smaller crates are used for the tea. These crates reflect the farm fresh product, authenticating that the apple products are just like fresh picked apples from the orchard.

The red teapot is made to look like an apple with the tag in the shape of a leaf, which is the inspiration for the logo design. This apple shape is also used in the teabags with a leaf tag at the end. Line illustrations of the tea flavours show off the different tastes and the drawing style is simple and comforting.
