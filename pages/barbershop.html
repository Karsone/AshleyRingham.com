The Daily Barbershop

Logo + Packaging

Description

The daily barbershop is a fictional company with a collection of menâ€™s hair care products. As this is a fictional company branding and identity needed to be established in order to create the packaging.

Target Audience

The audience for this product is men, ages 24-40, mid-range income, who value their hair style, and have an affinity for the barbershop experience.

Design Solution

The branding takes inspiration from the vintage barbershop. The colours: blue, white and red, derive from the barbershop pole, however used in modern, darker hues. The stripes also stem from this barber pole and their angled pattern illuminate movement. The logo itself is a badge, incorporating classic vintage style that the audience appreciates.
