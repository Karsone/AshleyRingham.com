Cocow Cows

Branding + Packaging

Description

A fictional company I developed from the childlike idea that brown cows make chocolate milk. The concept behind the company is chocolate bars made with different colour spots to give the appearance of cow print.

Target Audience

The target audience is primarily female age 10â€“25, quirky personality. Spend disposable income hanging out with friends which usually leads to hanging out eating junk food and candy.

Design Solution

Different cow illustrations mirror the colours of the individual flavours.The illustrations themselves are flat and simplistic made in the same style for consistency. These different cows are used as alternate logo marks to suit different packaging and branding needs. I made the chocolate as well and colour matched them to go with the packaging.
