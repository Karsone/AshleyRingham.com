Once Upon a Pantone Smoothie

Publication + Typography

Description

This is a collaborative project with CÃ©line Chuang. We both discussed an interest in food styling and photography, influenced by colour, we came up with the idea to create a publication that flaunts the visual appeal of food, or in this case smoothies. We took it one step further and decided to create promotional pieces to market a fictional book launch.

Group Work

We collectively styled and shot the images, took turns editing photos, stories and coming up with clever puns. My personal duties were designing the book cover and book layout, as well as creating the postcards, althought we had separtate duties we were both involved in the design process of all aspects of this project.

Design Solution

We wanted it to be tongue and cheek in the literal and figurative sense, combining bright colours, fun stories, clean layouts and delicious smoothies. The fruit was styled and shot on a black background, allowing the colour to pop. Arranged in a landscape grid to suit the publication size. Layout kept simple with lots of white space, to allow the focus to maintain on the images.
