About Me

Hello! I am a graphic designer from Vancouver, BC. I specialize in branding, illustration, packaging, and web design, and have a love for photography and telling stories through my work. Since childhood I have always loved creating things, from paper dolls, to mini magazines and children's books, there was always something I could make. I still have the love of creating today. I take inspiration from wordplay, stories, cheeky ideas, and experimenting with colour.I'm always ready for a fun project with fun people, so let's work together and create something awesome!
