I am a graphic designer from Vancouver, Canada. I specialize in branding, illustration, packaging, and web design, and have a love for photography and telling stories through my work. When I was small I could always be caught making things: from paper dolls, to mini magazines and children's books, I aways had an idea and a way to make it happen. I still have the love of creating today, and the passion to figure things out. I take inspiration from wordplay, cheeky ideas, and experimenting with colour. I believe in working hard, being nice and having fun. If you would like to work together please reach out, even if it's just to chat I would love to meet with you!
